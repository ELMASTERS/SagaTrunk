There are two different trader types namely 'single' and 'grouped'. A single trader has a user-interface which shows 5 production and 5 supplement items. A grouped trader show a user-interface with maximum 25 subtrades with 1 production item and maximum 2 supplement items. In the example we show a trader. The trader shows submenu '11' for display options after looking this up in the client we see "Alter Stone : Beholder Eyes + 4;Alter Stone : Bonecrush Rain + 4;Alter Stone : Burning Anger + 4;Alter Stone : Dragon Blade Crusher + 3;Alter Stone : Coldheart + 4;Alter Stone : Magic Charm + 4;" which is associated with "57;61;65;69;73;77;". This means that when you press on the submenu "Alter Stone : Beholder Eyes + 4;Alter Stone : Bonecrush Rain + 4" it will open trade with id listed as "57". As we take a close look at our example. In production we have 1 item and in supplement 3 items listed for id 57. This will mean you gain that one item and require those three items. The group attribute for a 'single' trade can be leftout. For readabillity/consistancy in the fileformat we give it a default value of zero. The refund attribute means the amount of rufi the trader will give you after the trade.
